New Heart Stories

Ariel

A powerful testimony of deep healing and transformation through New Heart 54…

×

Ariel

In May 2025, God opened a beautiful new chapter as we launched New Heart Kids #1 in Los Angeles. It was a long-awaited dream within the New Heart community to see all generations blessed through New Heart, and it was incredible to witness how God opened the door to make this dream a reality. When I first joined New Heart Kids, my heart was especially for young parents. As a mother of four, I remembered the seasons when I felt spiritually dry and left out while raising my little ones. I wanted to serve in a way that would give parents space to be renewed and restored in God’s presence. But as I served, God expanded my heart to embrace their children as well. Serving about twenty children, from toddlers to preteens, I saw firsthand how deeply God treasures each one of these young children. His desire is to raise and equip them as future leaders within the New Heart community. There were certainly challenging moments, but through it all, I witnessed both parents and children receiving one new heart through the Holy Spirit by the end of those three days. With that same heart and encouragement, I was overjoyed to hear that we had New Heart Kids #2 in Korea in October. I believe this is God’s confirmation of His will to call us as a ‘family’ and to give His New Heart to all generations.

Keziah

I received deep inner restoration and have made a firm decision to serve and draw even closer to God. I also chose to forgive my ex-husband for everything in the past and even in the present. The accusations and hatred that I carried for many years have now transformed into something new that leads me to pray even more. I have truly experienced transformation in both my heart and my spirit. Before joining the mission, I had carried pain in my heart for more than 18 years. But now, that burden has become so much lighter, and I can truly feel that I am no longer carrying it the way I once did. “NEW 54” has been a great blessing and a transformative experience for both my heart and my spirit. I also desire to become a blessing to the brothers and sisters who will join the next generation of the New Heart mission.

Han Ye-seul

A testimony of faithful service, spiritual renewal, and God’s sustaining grace…

Han Ye-seul

Hello. My name is Han Ye-seul, and I served as a team member for the first time in the 54th session, having previously participated as a candidate in the 51st session. Serving on the praise team, even while pregnant, I found God continually strengthening me. He reminded me of Mary and Martha—that above all, He wants me to sit at His feet and listen. In the midst of serving, He poured His heart into mine. Though I worried about my physical condition, God sustained me with supernatural rest. Serving became joy, and I encourage all candidates to join the team in the future. If given the opportunity again, I will prepare with prayer and wholehearted dedication for the souls God entrusts to us.
