Ariel

My sister's profound experience with New Heart 52 in Israel had a deep impact on me. Witnessing her transformation and her confession of faith in Jesus to our mother inspired me to attend New Heart in the USA in May 2025. I was born into a devout Orthodox Jewish family, alongside my sister and our triplet brother. Our upbringing was steeped in the traditions and teachings of our faith, and for many years, this was the foundation of my religion. However, as I grew older, a sense of unease began to stir within me. Despite my fervent prayers and commitment to our religious practices, I found myself yearning for a deeper connection, a more profound experience of God's peace and love, which I felt was lacking. This spiritual yearning led me to seek guidance, and I prayed earnestly for God to lead me to the truth. About six years ago, my journey took a transformative turn. I began to research Jesus, approaching the subject with an open mind and a sincere desire to understand. What I discovered during this period of intense study and reflection was nothing short of revelatory. I delved into the prophecies of the Hebrew Bible and was astonished to see how clearly they pointed to Jesus as the long-awaited Messiah. The intricate details, the historical accuracy, and the profound theological implications of these prophecies resonated deeply within me. It was a profound awakening, confirming a truth that had been quietly stirring in my heart. This journey of discovery solidified my belief in Jesus, forever changing my spiritual path. Getting permission to leave Israel during my army service, especially with the ongoing war, was challenging. We booked the flight, prayed earnestly, and by God's grace, I received permission just one day before I departed for the USA. The New Heart program was a period of immense grace and personal change. Over three days, I grew closer to God by spending undistracted time with Him, a profoundly enriching experience. I am grateful to God for transforming my life and giving me a new heart during New Heart 53#.
