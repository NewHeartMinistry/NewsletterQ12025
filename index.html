Editorial

God's Faithfulness: Our Enduring Foundation

New Heart Ministry recently celebrated its 54th gathering in Korea, marking almost two decades of God's sustaining grace. The name "New Heart" itself was divinely inspired, and the first gathering under this banner took place in Cambodia, serving young Vietnamese students. Since then, the ministry has expanded its reach to China, Ethiopia, Israel, the Philippines, Vietnam, and Korea, with its headquarters in the United States. In a beautiful orchestration of God's heart for every generation, New Heart has also grown to include New Heart Kids programs, allowing children to join the gatherings with dedicated activities specifically designed for them. This year alone, two such programs have already taken place—one in the USA and one in Korea—underscoring the importance of every generation in the Kingdom Family.

Across these nations, we have seen God's faithfulness as He transforms lives—bringing souls from death to life, placing within them a New Heart (Ezekiel 36:26), and awakening them to their God-given purpose and destiny—to raise a people of fire. Despite differences in culture, location, and language, the Holy Spirit has intricately woven us together into one family: The Kingdom Family. We celebrate and honor one another according to His calling, holding nothing back as we serve each other with transformed hearts (Acts 2).

As God's vision for New Heart Ministry continues to sharpen, we sense His desire not only to document the mighty works and the abundant grace flowing through this ministry but also for us, as a family, to nurture our connections and care for one another. Thus, the Newsletter for New Heart Family. We believe that, as promised in Isaiah 54, God is preparing to expand New Heart—not only into new physical territories but also through spiritual elevation and enlargement. By His strength and Spirit, we are committed to fulfilling what He has declared according to His covenant over this ministry in the days ahead.

From the Founders

Shalom, New Heart Family! Usually, we greet each other with "How are you?" meaning, "How's your health, how's your family, and how's your work?" Has anyone asked you, instead, "How is your heart?" Maybe it's more fitting for us. To God, it's always a matter of the heart.

When God sent Samuel to Jesse's house to choose Israel's next king, He said: "For not as man sees does the LORD see, because man looks at the outward appearance, but the LORD looks at the heart." This is especially important since we are the New Heart community: "And I will give you a new heart, and a new spirit I will put within you. And I will remove the heart of stone from your flesh and give you a heart of flesh."

This is our prayer and our confident hope in faith in Jesus and the gift of grace—that your hearts are in good condition, whatever your circumstances may be. We praise and thank our Father for existing in the power of the Holy Spirit every victorious Wednesday. God bless you, your family, and your labor of love for God's people and His community.

— Jason & Youngmi Cha
New Heart Global Directors
