Editorial

New Heart exists to help sons and daughters of God encounter His presence, receive healing, be restored, and be sent out with purpose. We rejoice in what the Lord is doing this season as our family continues to grow globally.

The Fourth Day reminds us that Jesus is always walking with us — even when we don’t recognize Him at first. Our heart is to see every believer strengthened for the journey ahead, renewed in spirit, and grounded in His love.
