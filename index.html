4th Day Conversations — Fall 2025

"I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you; I will remove from you your heart of stone and give you a heart of flesh." — Ezekiel 36:26

Founders' Greeting

Founders and New Heart Global Directors

Hello New Heart Community!

Usually we greet each other by saying, "How are you?" That is, how is your health, how is your family, and how is your work. But has anyone ever asked you instead, "How is your heart?" Perhaps that would be a more appropriate question or greeting for us, because with God, it is always about the heart.

When God sent the prophet Samuel to the house of Jesse to choose the next King of Israel, God established His principle when He said, The Lord does not look at the things people look at. People look at the outward appearance, but the Lord looks at the heart. This is especially significant and relevant because we are the New Heart community.

God says, I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you; I will remove from you your heart of stone and give you a heart of flesh. It is our prayer and confident hope through the precious faith in our Lord Jesus and through the abundant gift of His grace, that your heart will be healthy, no matter your particular situation or circumstance.

We praise and thank our Abba Father for upholding us in the power of the Holy Spirit through each victorious 4th day. God bless you, your family and your labor of love for God's people and His church.

With love,
Pastors Jason & Youngmi Cha
Founders and New Heart Global Directors
