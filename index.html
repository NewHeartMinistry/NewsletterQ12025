Greeting

Hello, New Heart Family! Normally, we greet one another by saying, "How are you?" That is to say, how is your health, how is your family, and how is your work. Has anyone asked you, instead, "How is your heart?" Perhaps that is more appropriate for us. For God, it is always a matter of the heart. When God sent Samuel to Jesse's house to select the next king of Israel, He said, "The LORD does not look at the things people look at. People look at the outward appearance, but the LORD looks at the heart." This is particularly important because we are a New Heart community: "I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you; I will remove from you your heart of stone and give you a heart of flesh." It is our prayer and confident hope through faith in Jesus and the gift of grace that your heart is well, whatever your circumstances. We praise and thank our Abba Father for sustaining us in the power of the Holy Spirit through each victorious 4th day. God bless you, your family, and your labor of love for God's people and His church. — Jason & Youngmi Cha, Founders & New Heart Global Directors
